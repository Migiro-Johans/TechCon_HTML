History

TechCon 2024, hosted by the Booth Technology Group at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business in May 2024, centered on the theme “Embracing Disruption Differentiation in the Age of Automated Everything.” The event brought together around 200 attendees and 28 industry leaders for a dynamic program featuring keynotes from workplace futurist Marti Konstant and DarioHealth CPO Eitan Shay, who spoke on career agility and product velocity. AI-focused workshops led by Manny Bernabe and Kevin Lee introduced business leaders to practical applications of custom GPTs and language models. Panel discussions explored automation in supply chains and the evolving startup and VC landscape in Chicago. The conference aimed to equip participants with strategic foresight, AI fluency, and networking opportunities, inspiring innovation and agile career development in a rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024, hosted by the University of Chicago Booth Technology Group, was to serve as a dynamic forum for exploring the technologies reshaping the modern world, engaging with the innovators leading that transformation, and celebrating Chicago’s emergence as a growing tech hub. The conference sought to promote technical awareness, build strategic connections, and inspire innovation by bringing together students, industry leaders, and academics to examine automation, AI, and career agility in the age of disruption
